About US:

* Located in Yavatmal district of Maharashtra, Sivkamal D. Ed College started its first academic session in 2006 with the view to impart quality education t the aspirants. The educational institution is affiliated to Maharashtra State Council of Educational Research & Training, Pune (Maharashtra SCERT, Pune) and is recognized by National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) Bhopal. Working under the guidance and sponsorship of Shahu Shikshan Sanstha, Pandharpur, the college conducts Diploma in Education (D. Ed) course in Marathi medium.
*Sivkamal D. Ed College is a co-educational college, which intends to produce competent teachers. In order to attain its aim, the college boasts of being fully equipped with all the mandatory facilities.
